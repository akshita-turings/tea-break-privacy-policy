Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tea Break, a social interaction application developed and managed by TURINGS XYZ PRIVATE LIMITED ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Username, phone number, email address (if provided)
  • Profile Information: Avatar selection, profile picture, user preferences
  • Payment Information: UPI ID for receiving payments (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Content: Audio recordings, text messages, comments, suggestions, and other content you create or share

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, time spent in rooms
  • Network Information: IP address, network type, connection status
  • Audio Data: Voice recordings during live discussions and debates (processed in real-time)

2.3 Permissions We Request

Important: Our app requires certain permissions to function properly. We only use these permissions for the stated purposes:

  • RECORD_AUDIO: To enable voice participation in live discussions and debates. Audio is processed in real-time and may be temporarily stored for quality purposes.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To send you notifications about room updates, messages, and app activities.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To allow you to upload images for rooms and save content.
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES: To access images you select for room thumbnails and profile pictures.
  • READ_CONTACTS: To help you invite friends to join discussions (optional feature).
  • READ_PHONE_STATE: To verify your phone number and ensure account security.
  • BLUETOOTH / BLUETOOTH_CONNECT: To enable audio output through Bluetooth devices.
  • INTERNET / NETWORK_STATE: To connect to our servers and provide app functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve our services, including live audio discussions and debates
  • User Authentication: To verify your identity and secure your account
  • Communication: To send you notifications, updates, and respond to your inquiries
  • Payment Processing: To process payments and earnings through UPI
  • Content Moderation: To ensure a safe and respectful environment for all users
  • Analytics: To understand app usage patterns and improve user experience
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Audio Recording and Processing

Special Notice Regarding Audio Data:

Our app uses the RECORD_AUDIO permission to enable live voice discussions. When you participate in audio rooms:

  • Your voice is captured and transmitted in real-time to other participants
  • Audio may be temporarily stored on our servers for quality assurance and moderation purposes
  • We do not permanently store your voice recordings unless required for legal or safety purposes
  • You can control your microphone access through your device settings at any time
  • Audio data is encrypted during transmission

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third-party service providers who assist in app operations (payment processors, cloud storage, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Safety and Security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of users or others
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to users)
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share your information

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Permission Control: Manage app permissions through your device settings

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Our app is not intended for users under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.
