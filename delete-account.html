Data Deletion Request

Last Updated: November 2024

Your Right to Data Deletion

At Tea Break, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

This page provides you with a simple way to request the deletion of your account and all personal information associated with it.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you request account deletion, we will permanently delete the following information:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, phone number, profile details
  • Profile Data: Avatar, profile picture, user preferences, settings
  • Content: Audio recordings, text messages, comments, suggestions, circles
  • Activity Data: Room participation history, engagement records, earnings history
  • Social Data: Followers, following lists, friend connections
  • Payment Information: UPI ID and payment history (as per legal requirements)
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, preferences, and analytics data

Note: Some data may be retained for legal or regulatory compliance purposes, such as transaction records required by law. However, this data will be anonymized and not associated with your identity.

How to Request Data Deletion

Method 1: Email Request (Recommended)

Send an email to our support team with the following information:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Your registered phone number or email address
  • Your username (if available)
  • Confirmation that you want to delete your account

Email Address:

developers@turings.xyz

Method 2: In-App Request

You can also request account deletion directly from within the Tea Break app:

  1. Open the Tea Break app
  2. Go to your Profile/Settings
  3. Navigate to Account Settings
  4. Select "Delete Account" or "Request Data Deletion"
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions

Processing Time

Once we receive your deletion request, we will:

  • Verify your identity to ensure the request is legitimate
  • Process your request within 30 days
  • Send confirmation once your data has been deleted

We may contact you during this process to verify your identity and confirm your request.

Important Information

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your account will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered
  • You will lose access to all your data, content, and earnings
  • You will need to create a new account if you wish to use Tea Break again
  • Any pending payments will be processed according to our payment policy

Data Retention Exceptions

We may retain certain information for legal or regulatory purposes, including:

  • Transaction records required by financial regulations
  • Information required for legal compliance or dispute resolution
  • Anonymized data used for analytics and service improvement

All retained data will be anonymized and not associated with your identity.

Third-Party Services

Please note that if you have shared content or data through third-party services (such as social media platforms), you may need to request deletion from those services separately.

Your Privacy Rights

In addition to data deletion, you have the following privacy rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Restriction: Request restriction of data processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided above.
